Concept Superhero: Bait (Set in DC Comics Universe)

No link to read until Evilynhehe finishes it and gives me her opinion of it!

Background Before Reading (If You Want Before Diving In)

Inspiration

I'd say after watching two seasons of Young Justice, the idea of Bait started to form in my head and I started writing it out. It does follow the first two seasons loosely before branching out to its own thing. So of course, spoiler alert if you plan to watch Young Justice. Or watch it first and go back to this. Did alter and add some stuff from the beginning of season 3. Other sources of inspiration that helped shaped Bait came from numerous Batman comics, DC movies, real life, etc.

Structure

First, it's not a comic book or a book. I think it's more like a script encompassing the general overview of the character. As stated above, it's based loosely on the Young Justice TV show. As for "explicit content", I don't believe it's that bad but read at your own risk.

Timeline of this Script

I started it on October 2021 and finished it finally on May 2022.

Why Did I Write This?

Believe or not, it was to stimulate my brain while writing my graduate school applications. Writing application essays are not exciting, and oftentimes I hit a wall on what to write. So, to stimulate myself, I started writing this. It helped and soon, I thought I could create a synopsis for a DC character. It became my favorite pastime for a while because it was fun writing my ideas into words and developing it into a story.

Drawings?

No drawings, unfortunately. Even the title page or symbol for the character. I'm atrocious at drawing, and I know there should be an idea of what my character looks like. Or anything that is going on in the book since it's supposed to be a comic. But I suppose you can use your own imagination.

After Reading Thoughts

Will Bait Be Part Of DC Comics?

If you liked the script and want to see Bait be part of DC Comics, I'm flattered and I thank you. If you didn't like it, all good. Either way, thanks for taking the time to read it. But honestly, I don't think so. First, with all the characters DC has, Bait is probably not unique. I don't know every character in DC though and this one may be redudant (is that how you spell it. There's no spellchecker in HTML!). Second, perhaps his story is boring, reptitive, and offer no new value for DC Comics. Third, I have no ideal way of pitching this idea and I don't want waste their time on a likely boring character idea. But if I do bump into someone from DC Comics accidentally, I probably will ask him/her to take a look. When I wrote this, it was for fun and at no point during writing I was thinking about this idea being picked up. As you can probably tell, writing is not my forte but this was for fun and not for anyone or anything. But I won't deny it would be cool if this character idea resonates well. Even if I like it, it's not good to push this onto other people. So I leave this here for others who want to read and see an idea.

Bait's Role

I really hesistate calling Bait a superhero. Superhero always means there are superpowers, but Bait doesn't have them. He does have some exceptional, yet mundane, qualities, such as being an expert marksman and great at hand to hand combat. I tried to portray him as someone who is cold and never takes anything personal when doing crime fighting but I don't think I achieved that. Also, very level-headed and is there to help defuse situations first. And to make himself the "bait" or like setting up traps. Pivoting to what I said above, if Batman is considered a superhero, then Bait I guess he can be a minor superhero. True to his heritage, he is someone who works in the shadows. Also very loyal to the Batman and will always do his best to be avaliable if called on. Mixture of Alfred and Robin's relationship to Batman.

Additional Bait Story Ideas
  • I think the one I really wanted was him facing off the Batman Who Laughs. Feels that enemy suits him well but I didn't incorporate it because I thought the book was getting way too long. And if I was, I want to read all the comics that BWL is in and devise a story based off that.
  • An event set in cyberpunk setting. Cyberpunk 2077, Blade Runner, Blade Runner 2049, and others piqued my interest in the cyberpunk genre. Perhaps it's the inclusion of noir element as well that makes it appealing.
